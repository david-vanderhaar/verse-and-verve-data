peel back the layers of my eyes
and see.
rub away the palm prints on my hands
and behold.
I am a child of the wild place.
I am returning to my plot
of peace,
of dust
and mud.

pick your teeth with
my bones.
smile as you fade
into nothing.
creep into yourself.
wreak
chaos.
diminish
forever.
