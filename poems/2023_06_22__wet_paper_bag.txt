title: Wet Paper Bag
created: 2023-06-22
END_METADATA

This feeling.
Put words to it.
Don't let it slink back into
you.

Lick the roof of your mouth.
Spit each word
so it sticks
like a wet paper bag
to the parking lot.

Leave your words there
on the steaming asphalt.

Or
let the feeling flee.

It will be back
anyway.
