title: a dry land
created: 2024-09-22
END_METADATA

I eat the long time of my youth
in return for a fat belly
and a view of the spectacle.

Death at the bottom 
of a dry land.

Leave it to the youthful to
beat at death's door
and demand he open up.

Leave it to the wise to
explain why death isn't at home today. 

"He is out,
working the earth".
