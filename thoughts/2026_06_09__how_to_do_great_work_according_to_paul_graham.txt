According to Paul Graham

There are four steps to do great work:
1. Find a field
2. Get to the frontier
3. Find the gaps iin research, the unanswered and/or unasked questions
4. explore those gaps.

The rest follows.

There are four factors of doing great work
1. Curiosity
2. Ability
3. Effort
4. Luck

Curiosity can be a primary driver to succeed and discover. It can give you the energy to work hard, practice, increase ability, increase effort, and manage your body and your soul well.

Luck is luck, we can recognize it's existence and then ignore it from there.
